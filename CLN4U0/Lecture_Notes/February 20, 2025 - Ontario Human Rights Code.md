| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| A perceived monolith of a group of people (usually minorities) based on prejudices. | Biases, typically negative, pertaining to a group of people. | Differential treatment based on a quality or characteristic. |
